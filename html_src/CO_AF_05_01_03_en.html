Environment Management System (ISO14001)
  • Business system where the corporate body evaluates its own environment management and impact and set concrete goals to implement through the PDCA cycle of PLAN → DO → CHECK → ACT
  • Incheon International Airport Corporation manages and directs the reduction of environmental impact in all areas of airport operation and construction through applying the certified management system
Environment Management System Certification
  • Incheon International Airport Corporation received the Environment Management System (ISO14001) License from Lloyd’s QA (England) in 1998 for airport construction and in 2002 for airport operations. Through appropriate implementation of environmental goals, key environmental impact evaluation, and internal environmental assessment and training, IIAC has met the ISO requirements and also abides by the environment management system through external evaluation (yearly) and re-licensing assessment (once every 3 years). IIAC has also adopted the improved environmental outcome assessment in 2012 for a stable operation of the Environment Management System.
