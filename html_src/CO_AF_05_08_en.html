Incheon Airport Resource Management

All waste from airport operation are largely recycled, incinerated, or buried. Recycling process includes sorting, crushing, and selecting of re-usable material, and waste that cannot be recycled are incinerated or sent to landfill. Incheon International Airport Corporation works to effectively handle waste and maximize recycling at the resource sorting facility and materials that can be recovered through incineration is proceed in the resource recovery facility. All wastes from the airport pass through the two management facilities.

Resource recovery facility

All recyclable waste from airport operation is sorted and effectively processed at the resource recovery facility. Part of the inflammable wastes are incinerated and the waste heat generated from incineration are resold or used to operate the incineration facility. All contaminants produced by the facility are measured by the Tele Monitoring System (TMS), and the result of the measurement is made available in real time to the Korean Environmental Protection Agency and local governments in the vicinity.

Resource sorting facility

Resource sorting facility processes all other waste that cannot be processed through the resource recovery facility, and it has been operating since 2010. From the waste’s entry to its exit, there is a strict surveillance of each processing stage, and the type and amount of each waste is monitored at all times. There also is the effort to prioritize identifying and processing recyclable wastes to reduce processing cost and promote an environmental-friendly airport management.
