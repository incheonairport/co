Aircraft Noise Monitoring Station Operation

Incheon International Airport Corporation operates Aircraft Noise Monitoring Station to accurately analyze noise impact resulting from airport operation.

Noise Level Monitoring Station Location
Noise Level Monitoring Station
Analysis and reduction recommendation of noise level measurement results
  • Data collected from each monitoring station is sent to the Incheon Airport Environment Monitoring Office, and the Environment Monitoring Office monitors the measurement results in real time.
  • Areas without a stationary monitoring station is studied using the noise level measurement vehicle.
  • Additionally, there is a aircraft noise level impact research service every 5 years. The most recent service took place in 2014, and the integrity of the measurement was confirmed by a professional measurement entity.
  • There is continual effort to reduce aircraft noise through consistent and comprehensive data collection and analysis, and all results are submitted to the Seoul Regional Aviation Administration for future reference.
  • Visit the Incheon Airport website or National Noise Information System website for measurement results collected from the monitoring stations.
