Field Trip Info

Incheon International Airport provides Airport Field Trip Program for our customers.

Field Trip Info

Dates and times

  • Possible days: Daily
  • Possible times: Part 1 (10:00) Part 2 (15:00)

Field trip lasts for about 1 hour.

Request Period

  • anytime between 1-30 days before the desired date

Requests will be filled on a first-come first-served basis.

Participant and group size

  • Participants : children over 5 years of age, middle/high school students, adults
  • Group size : groups between 10-80 people

Please call the Volunteer Officer(032-741-3555) for a smaller group or an individual tour.

Things to Note

  • When using a group bus, park temporary to unload by Floor 3 Exit 1 and load by Floor 1 Exit 14, and during the field trip, park in the Parking Lot.
  • Please arrive on time and contact us the day before if there are any changes to the plan.
  • The tour course might change depending on airport security, construction, and other airport management related conditions.
  • Direct all other inquiries to the Incheon Airport Volunteer Team Office(032-741-3555).
