Incheon Airport Water Resource Management
Wastewater Reclamation and Reusing System
  • Incheon International Airport manages and reuses all wastewater and runoff from the airport area at the wastewater management facility located within the International Business Complex, which can process up to 30,000 tons/day.
  • Water filtered through the wastewater recycling facility is reused in restrooms, for cooling machinery, and to water the gardens, and part of the filtered water is released into the ocean through a reservoir.
Location of wastewater management facility
Rainwater disposal – oil separation facility

There are 5 oil separation facility within the airport to process rainwater.

The collected rainwater is treated to ensure that there is less than 5ppm level of oil before being released.

DE-ICING Wastewater disposal

De-icing is a method of getting rid of any ice or frost on the aircraft’s surface that can inhibit safe operation during takeoff. In order to minimize the effect on the environmental from de-icing, there are a total of 8 De-icing Pads to the south of the Passenger Terminal, north of aircraft house, and south of the Cargo Terminal. The waste liquid from de-icing is collected and sent to a professional processing company, and there is continual effort to ensure that there are no additional effect on the environment.
