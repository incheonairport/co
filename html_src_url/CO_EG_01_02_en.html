Safety Goal Items and Controlled Targets
공공데이터 제공 책임관 및 실무 담당자 안내 표
Safety Goal Type Detailed Control Target
Ground Safety Accidents
  1. 1. When there is a collision between the aircraft on board and the vehicle (equipment) or object (excluding aircraft in operation)
  2. 2. When there is a collision between a vehicle and a vehicle, equipment, personnel, or facility in the Transit Area, OR a personnel is injured or vehicle, equipment, facility is damaged from a collision between a personnel and equipment
irport Facility Malfunction
  1. 1. The runway, taxiways, and moorings are severely damaged in such a way, interfering with the operation of the aircraft
  2. 2. When there is a fire in the aircraft within the Transit Area that may interfere with the operation of the aircraft
  3. 3. When there are findings of aircraft parts, structures, or dangerous goods within the Transit Area that are considered detrimental to the safety of the aircraft in operation
  4. 4. When there is a oil leakage during aircraft refueling that may delay the normal operation of the aircraft
  5. 5. If the operation of air traffic lights is suspended
