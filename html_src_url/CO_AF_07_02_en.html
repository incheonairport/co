Fair-Trade Compliance Program(CP)

CP is the internal standardization system voluntarily set up by corporations to follow fair-trade related laws. The voluntary evaluation and implementation of change in the case of actions that violate fair-trade agreements is a way to strengthen corporate competitiveness and fostering a corporate culture of upholding fair-trade regulations.

CP Vision and Strategy
Mission
Establish foothold as a world-class public corporation with ethical management and mutual growth management
Vision
Foster a corporate culture of voluntarily upholding fair-trade regulations
Strategy
Adopt CP system
Prevent violations and promote reforms through implementation in the workplace
System evaluation and expansion
Sustainable upward spiral
