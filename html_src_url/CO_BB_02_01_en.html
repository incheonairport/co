Ministry of Land, Transport and Maritime Affairs Airport Management Level 1 Airport Management Certificate

Incheon International Airport received the highest grade for airport management and received the Level 1 Airport Management Certificate from ICAO’s 33rd board meeting in November 2003 from the Ministry of Land, Transport and Maritime Affairs.

As such, Incheon International Airport has proven that it is managed according to the international standard set by ICAO, SARPs, and domestic standard set by AOS.

After receiving the Airport Management Certificate, the status is maintained by regular assessment by the government and through internal safety evaluation to uphold the safety standards for airport management.
