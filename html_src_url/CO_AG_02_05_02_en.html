Central Service Practice Standards
  • We will do our best to provide efficient boarding process for the convenience of our customers.
  • We will build trust by providing accurate and efficient security search for the safety of users and aircraft.
  • We will deliver comfort and fun to transfer passengers through quick transfer process, comfortable transfer facilities, and diverse programs.
  • We will make the baggage claim easy by adopting most up-to-date cargo transit system and accurate and quick cargo processing.
  • We will provide systematic transportation network for easy and safe access to the airport.
  • We will provide clean and convenience parking lot service.
  • We will provide friendly instructions and install customer-targeted signs to make it easier to get to the customer’s destination.
  • We will provide satisfactory service to customers using the shops within the airport.
  • We will maintain a clean facility for our customers through continual improvements.
Customer Service Practice Standards
  • We will run a 24-hour information desk to provide friendly service.
  • We will listen to our customers through e-mail, post, phone call (1577-2600), website, and other outlets.
  • We will train and take action in the case of a less than satisfactory service from our employee.
  • We will notify you of the reason and the expected solution date in the case exceeding normal processing time.
  • We will implement changes according to the recommendations on the ASQ and customer service survey.
  • We will adopt a claim submission and compensation system for harm due to the airport’s fault.
  • We will compensate for any damages related to parking lot use if the damage was due to the airport’s fault.
  • We will compensate an equivalent of 10,000 KRW when an unsatisfactory service is submitted and verified.
  • We will improve any shortcomings through self service monitoring (at least 2 times a year).
  • We will allow customer participation in management and service improvement through a customer participation-communication department.

  • We will implement any changes that are important and come from the VOC Committee.
  • We will enhance and develop our service by posting customer service status and incorporating customer opinion.
  • We will work to publicly disclose management information in a timely and accurate manner.
  • We will publicly disclose information to ensure the customers’ rights to know and transparency of management.
  • We will provide all procedure and facility information through the airport website and over the phone.
Request for customer cooperation
  • The airport is an important national facility and often require the cooperation of our customers for safety and orderly operation, so please be understanding even if there is an inconvenience.
  • Security search is a necessary step for airport security, and it might take a longer time in the case of heightened security in response to terrorism. Please be understanding of this fact.
  • Please follow the guidance of airport staff for a faster entry and exit through the airport.
  • Please be courteous as the airport is a public space used by people from all over the world.
  • Please share your praise and suggestions as they serve as the basis of improvement.
