Internal monitoring
  • Integrity Call System
    IIAC’s Auditing Department formed the “Integrity Call System” in 2011 to receive feedback from contractors about work transparency, suggestions, and comments about work procedure improvement.
  • Integrity Mileage System
    Incheon International Airport Corporation keeps track of department evaluation according to the key performance indicator(KPI) to motivate each team to participate in the promotion of the culture of ethics and integrity.
  • Executive staff integrity evaluation
    Incheon International Airport Corporation consults external research agencies to provide surveys to employees regarding the conduct of executive staff in order to self-evaluate the level of integrity and discover ways to enhance ethical conduct.
External monitoring
Public agency integrity evaluation
  • Objective evaluation of the public corporation’s integrity level
  • Evaluation agency: Anti-Corruption and Civil Rights Commission
  • Evaluation categories: internal integrity, external integrity, policy client evaluation, cases of corruption or other acts that harms trustworthiness
Anti-corruption competitiveness evaluation
  • Evaluation of public corporation’s anti-corruption and integrity policy and the practice of the policies
  • Evaluation agency: Anti-Corruption and Civil Rights Commission
  • Evaluation categories: will/efforts of anti-corruption, anti-corruption results, cooperation on implementation of anti-corruption measures
KoBex Index
  • conduct surveys about ethical management, including corporate social responsibility
  • Evaluation agency: Institute for Industrial policy Studies
  • Evaluation categories: management strategy, governance structure, partner businesses, community, environment, etc.
