Offline education
Workshop for ethics officers

Incheon International Airport Corporation conducts quarterly workshops geared towards each department’s ethics officer. We do our best to reveal ethical risks that might arise, reforms to reduce these risks, promote ethical practices at the department level, provide professional seminars, and expand ethical culture.

Ethics seminar for all staff

Incheon International Airport Corporation tries to foster a stronger ethical capacity for all employees through inviting expert lecturers.

Ethics forum by department

Incheon International Airport Corporation opens a forum each quarter to discuss ethics, transparency, integrity, anti-corruption, and other related topics within each department and to share the results of such open discussions to the Ethical Management Department for the enhancement of future ethics strategy.

Online education

We provide videos and education programs online about public corporation ethical management and integrity so that our employees can easily learn about ethical management, thereby increasing the effectiveness of ethical management.

Partner ethics manager education

Incheon International Airport Corporation provides quarterly workshops for our partners’ ethical manager in an effort to promote ethical culture in all agencies related to Incheon Airport.
