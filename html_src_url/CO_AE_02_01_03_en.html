Ethics Regulation

Incheon International Airport Corporation’s employees should abide by the Employee Code of Conduct and Ethics Charter. The Ethics Charter embodies IIAC’s basic values of ethical management, and the Employee Code of Conduct outlines the specific actions.

Officer Integrity Contract Management Regulation

Incheon International Airport Corporation’s officers are members of a public organization, and they are responsible for being good examples for a transparent and accountable business culture by acting according to the Integrity Contract and facing the consequences when in violation of these regulations.

Download
Guidelines for Reporting Crimes Related to Officers’ Work

Clearly regulates process of protection for the whistle blower and the severely punishment of the officer who has committed a crime related to his/her work in order to prevent corruption and fraudulent acts among employees.

Download
Management Manual for Reporting Procedure and Reporter Protection

Regulates the submission and processing of reports and reporter protection policies for the Incheon International Airport Corporation and, thereby, contribute to preventing harms to public good and enhancing whistle blower/reporter protection.
