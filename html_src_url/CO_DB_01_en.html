Overview

Development purpose

Develop international business and administrative facility to the South of Passenger Terminal to support Incheon International Airport’s work

Development method: BOOT
  • A private investor constructs the facility and gains back the investment by owning and managing the facility throughout the land use period (maximum 50 years)
  • The facility becomes the property of Airport Corporation at no cost after the land use period ends.
Development history
  • ‘95. Jul: International Business Complex IBC- Stage 1 City Development
  • ‘97. Feb: International Business Complex IBC- Stage 1 Established development plan
  • ‘97.Oct: International Business Complex IBC- Stage 1 Approve development plan(Ministry of Construction and Transportation)
  • ‘97.Nov: Begin private capital investment solicitation
