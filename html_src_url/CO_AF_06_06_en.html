Energy conservation campaigns and education

Incheon International Airport Corporation hosts energy conservation campaigns during the summer and winter season when energy-use increases, and these proactive activities include Coll-Biz Movement, rideshare promotion, sharing ways to conserve energy, getting energy conservation pledges, and producing and distributing promotional handheld fans. Every November, we hold a “Energy Conservation Month” events around the airport and raise awareness through billboard advertising, hanging banners, sharing advice for energy conservation, training all employees, and making announcements over the speakers. Incheon International Airport Corporation also invites an energy mascot from each department and partner offices for a quarterly workshop to raise awareness about the necessity of energy conservation and professional enhancements.
