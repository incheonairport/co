World’s first ICAO TRAINAIR PLUS Education Agency Certification

In March 2011, Incheon International Airport Corporation Talent Development Center became the first in the world to receive the UN-affiliated ICAO TRAINAIR PLUS Education Agency Certification.

ICAO TRAINAIR PLUS is ICAO’s educational agency evaluation and certification process to implement high-level education and training program to develop talents in the aviation industry.  

Incheon International Airport Corporation Talent Development Center is the first ICAO education agency that has successfully completed the ICAO TRAINAIR PLUS Education Agency Certification Evaluation in 9 areas including education process, education operation, lecturer, and for a total of 66 measures.

  Recently, we received an international endorsement as global professional aviation education center through the ICAO TRAINAIR PLUS Education Agency Certification.
