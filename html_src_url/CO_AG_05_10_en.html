Overseas Business Performance

Background

The purpose is to expand the airport development market to respond to world-wide increase in demand, and to export Incheon Airport’s management experience and expertise to foster global leadership in the airport section and sustainably increase business value.

Action plan
추진전략표
Stage 1 (-2013) Stage 2 (2013-2020) Stage 3 (2020-)
  • Established consulting business
  • Expanded consulting sectors
     (MP, FS, PM, CM)
  • Start investment/consign management business
  • Increase equity investment/high-value consulting
  • Acquire overseas airports/increase equity investment
  • Develop existing airport business/increase profit
Strategy for each business model
사업모델별 추진방안표
Consulting(airport construction) Master plan, feasibility study, construction audit, business management, test run, etc.
Consulting(airport operation) Improve service quality, airport marketing, increase public profit, IT service, flight safety system, airport security, etc.
Equity investment Invest in overseas airport as a way to expand business abroad and participate in airport management
Consignment management Consigned management of small airports and management participation through partnership with local organization
PPP Project Cooperative project with a global EPC group, airport management firm, participate as strategic investor (SI)
Overseas airport M&A Merger and acquisition (M&A) of small airports overseas with a potential to grow
Establish professional firm for overseas business management Establish a separate professional corporation to manage Incheon Airport’s overseas projects with sector-specific expertise
