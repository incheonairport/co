Leasing an Office

Lease of Business Facilities
  • Facilities used for offices, warehouses, counters, etc. for smooth operation of national institutions, airline, Resident Employee.
  • The basic contract period is two years (from March 1 to the end of February the following year)
Procedure for Contract Conclusion

The contract is concluded with via electronic contract, and it is necessary to join the electronic bidding system (If you are a member of a local market, you do not need to sign up separately)

  1. step 1

    Submit Lease
    Tenant→Corporation

  2. step 2

    Send a contract via E-mail
    (http://ebid.airportkr)
    Corporation→Tenant

  3. step 3

    Contract Confirmation
    & Approval: Tenants

  4. step 4

    Pay Security Deposit
    : Tenants

  5. step 5

    Sign a Contract
    (Printing Contract Possible)
    : Corporation

On-Site Inspection of Leasing Facilities (New Lease)

Key Agreement, Rental Space Cleaning Status, Facility Management Status, Space Location/Area Confirmation

Calculation Criteria of Security Deposit and Payment Method

6 months rent is calculated as rental deposit, and it is paid by cash to KEB 611-018036-301 Incheon International Airport Corporation (contract condition Article 10)
