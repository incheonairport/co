Expanding the adoption of renewable energy

Incheon International Airport Corporation is actively adoption renewable energy equipment such as solar power and geothermal power. Through the installation of 13 6,01kW solar power stations (2018 included), greenhouse emission has decreased annually by 3,124tco2 and 67 million KRW decrease in electricity bill annually. Additionally, the installation of 6 7,023kW geothermal power stations, greenhouse gas emission has been cut annually by 1,924tco2 41.2 million KRW decrease in electricity bill annually.

Through fuel cell development and invitation and installation of solar energy development facilities, we hope to contribute to the government’s “Implementation Plan for Renewable 3020”.
