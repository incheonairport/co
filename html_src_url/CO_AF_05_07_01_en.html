Incheon Airport Air Quality management

We monitor the air quality around the clock by installing measurement equipment in the Passenger Terminals and Concourse. Each quarter, we conduct the air quality of 34 additional locations in the Passenger Terminals, and the results are shared in a transparent manner through related agencies and the airport website. We have a internal standard that is stricter than that of the legal recommended level set by the “Interior Air Quality Management Act”, and we have a strong standard for filter change and special management in the case of yellow dust season.
