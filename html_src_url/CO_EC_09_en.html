ONE STOP Flight Support Service

ONE STOP Flight Support Service
  • New Flights Service: Incheon Airport will provide comprehensive information on transit area facilities, operating environment, precautions for operations, etc. to domestic and international air carriers that operate regularly or irregularly through Incheon Airport for the first time or after a certain period of time (more than 1 year).
  • Services for Newly Operating Aircraft: Airlines operating for the first-time or after a certain period of time (more than 1 year) at Incheon Airport that wish to fly ICAO aircraft rated C class or higher among scheduled and unscheduled commercial aircraft will be provided with Services. We will review the impacts of the airport operations beforehand so that aircraft can safely use and operate within the transit area facilities.

Service Procedure

New Airline Service Procedure

  • 01.Service provision notification for new carriers(Corporation→Airline)
  • 02.Negotiation of Service Provision(Corporation→Airline)
    • 1)Electronic File or 2)In-person Explanation (Transit area Tour Available)
  • 03.Commencement of Service(Corporation→Airline)
  • 04.Provide Airline Information(Airline→Corporation)
    • (New Service Information Document, Emergency Response Plan, etc.)
  • 05.Provide “ALL ABOUT SAFETY” Premium Service(Corporation→Airline)
    • (Ask any questions regarding flight safety within the first two months)

Newly Operated Aircraft Service Procedure

  • 01. Introduce New Flight Service Provision Notification for Airline(Corporation→Airline)
  • 02. Airport Capacity Provision of Information for Review(Airline→Corporation)
  • 03. Newly Operated Aircraft Airport Capacity Review(Corporation Subject)
  • 04. Provide Result of Airport Capacity Review(Corporation→Airline)
  • 05. Provide “ALL ABOUT SAFETY” Premium Service (Ask any questions regarding flight safety within the first two months)(Corporation→Airline)

Airport Capacity Review: Review the effects of the physical specifications and operational characteristics of the newly operated aircraft on airport operation to provide the optimal transit area facility and operating procedures.
