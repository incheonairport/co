Facility Introduction
  • Inha International Medical Center is a medical center located in Incheon Airport International Business Complex, the center of global network, and provides health services to foreign travelers visiting or traveling through Korea.
  • We hope you enjoy a trustworthy medical service as the Medical Center has doctors ready to treat patients from overseas, professional interpreters using various languages, best medical equipment, and customized services depending on the nationality of the patient (2 basement floors, 10 above-ground floors)
  • Departments: health check-up, plastic surgery, dermatology, dentistry, traditional eastern medicine, spa
    • health check-up : quick one-stop check-up program for transfer passengers, which can include cancer, neurological, and cardio disease screening and prevention programs.
    • dentistry : Inha International Medical Center’s dentistry strives for the most up-to-date equipment for your health and comfort. (teeth whitening, implant, cavity, and other treatments)
    • traditional eastern medicine : scientifically proven acupuncture procedure for weight loss and beauty care, other health programs to relieve your stress
