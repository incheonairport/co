On-site Business Approval

  • Approval Application

  • Approval Criteria/Condition/Cancel
On-Site Sales Disapproval Criteria
  • When the content of business can possibly harm public wellbeing
  • When the content of business can possibly be against the interests of passengers and the public
  • When the content of business is likely to infringe on the profits of the Corporation
  • When there are other reasons that are difficult to approve other sales activities
On-Site Sales Approval Criteria
  • Comply with aviation laws and airport facility management rules
  • Must comply when the Corporation require the application of the same criteria for safety management and cooperation on safety policy
  • Not act against the order of the airport and the convenience of passengers and the public
  • No transfer, lending, or entrustment of on-site sales . However, to entrust due to inevitable reason, must get a prior approval from the Corporation
  • Conduct business within an approved range, and when adding or changing business categories, must get a prior approval from the Corporation
  • Pay the amount of on-site sales or its equivalent according to the usage fee charge criteria of the Corporation and cooperate with the provision of information such as the sales revenue for calculating the fee for use
On-Site Sales Approval Rejection
  • A risk of harming a fair social order
  • Contrary to the interests of passengers and the public
  • Against the interests of the Corporation
  • Disturbance to the operation of the airport
  • A reason difficult to approve other sales activities
On-Site Sales Approval Cancellation
  • When On-Site Sales Approval conditions are violated
  • When the airport facility of the Corporation is destroyed or damaged either intentionally or in grave error
  • When sentenced to bankruptcy
  • When On-Site Sales Approval Cancellation Application is submitted
  • When on-site sales activities are inactive for more than 3 months with no valid justification
Unauthorized Business Act or Eviction and Unjust Enrichment
  • Use of airport facilities managed and operated by the Corporation for sales without receive On-Site Sales Approval
  • Use of airport facilities managed and operated by the Corporation for sales that do not fall within the scope of On-Site Sales Approval
  • Continued use of airport facilities managed and operated by the Corporation after the cancellation of On-Site Sales Approval, expiration of On-Site Sales Approval, and etc.
On-Site Sales Approval Objection

If you file a complaint against the rejection of On-Site Sales Approval Request, the notification will be sent within 10 days of receiving the complaint.
