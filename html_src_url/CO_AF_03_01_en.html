Incheon International Airport Corporation is growing as a global professional enterprise that is respected for its act of service.

Today, social responsibility is no longer a choice but a requirement for business management. Social contributions that meet the issues and needs of the stakeholder is key to sustainable growth and raises the value of the corporation. Incheon International Airport Corporation ties social contributions to the broader vision and strategy and hope to grow as a respected global professional enterprise in the field of aviation.
