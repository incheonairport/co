Countermeasure for Noise

Incheon International Airport Corporation is implementing airport noise level countermeasures, targeting Bukdo-myeon region, which is within the impact area. We provide soundproofing and climate control systems in residential buildings, pay for TV license fee, and operate Noise Level Countermeasure Committee with stakeholders and community members to continually support countermeasures.

Status of countermeasures for noise
Soundproofing construction

This project replaces existing exterior doors and windows to soundproof, double-paned window and soundproofing door, and all cost is covered by the IIAC.

Soundproofing construction impact

After the soundproofing construction, regular environmental noise including aircraft noise is reduced to provide an ideal living condition for conversations, phone calls, watching TV, reading, and sleeping, and the soundproofing construction provides the added benefit of insulation.
