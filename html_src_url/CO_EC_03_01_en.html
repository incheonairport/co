On-site Sales Approval Application

Anyone who wants to operate the facilities managed and operated by Incheon International Airport Corporation can conduct the business only after receiving approval from the Corporation following the approval procedure.

  1. step 1

    Submit Business Application Form

  2. step 2

    Business Approval

  3. step 3

    Issue Certificate of
    On-site Sales Approval

  • STEP1 Submit On-site Business Approval Application FormDownload
    • Attached Documents
      • One work plan for on-site sales within the Incheon International Airport
      • Documents of proof of permission, authorization, or notification of an administrative agency
      • Relevant contractual documents, If there is a contractual relationship with a residing business (institution)
      • Documents proving the need of sales activities within other airport areas (One copy of business registration certification)

      * Submit above Application Form and Attached Documents to the relevant department (facility management /operation department)

      * If you need confirmation, please contact the facility management and operation department.

      * Submit the Application Form to the leasing department when you lease the facility, if you want to lease facilities such as buildings, land, etc..

  • STEP2 We will review the feasibility and appropriateness of the intended sales and decide whether or not to approve the application.
  • STEP3 On-Site Sales Approval Certificate will be issued if approved of business.
    • Store On-site Sales Approval Certificate, which approves and certifies conduct of sales.
    • The certificate shall be renewed every year and the renewal application shall be composed through the same procedure before the expiration of the approval period.
