Reception Desk/Street Light Banners

Reception Desks
  • Facility Description: Short-term lease for reception desks for passengers arriving at Incheon International Airport
  • Leasing Purpose: Information on international conventions and events held in Republic of Korea
  • Location: East, West, and Center on the 1st floor of the Terminal
  • Only reception desks and signage board will be leased. Other items (chairs, phones, etc.) should be brought and managed by the lessee.
  • User Compliance - Familiarize with the Terms of Use of the Facility
    • No installation of additional facilities such as X banners and subsidiary crops, except given signage board.
    • After Termination of Use, non-execution of dismantling and recovering the condition of facilities may result in disadvantage to lease facilities in the future.
  • Signage (signboard)
    • Self-made, installed, and demolished with acrylic and foam boards
    • 17, 18, 19: width(2,000mm) x length (330mm) x thickness (2~3mm)
    • 36, 44, 61: width (1,470mm) x length(330mm) x thickness (2~3mm)
  • Related Inquiries
    • Internet (LAN) Inquiry: TEL. 032-741-0000 [If necessary, 7 days in advance]
    • Phone Line Installation Inquiry: Special Communication Room TEL. 032-741-7000 [At least, 7 days of receipt]
    • Group Bus Parking/Stop Inquiry: Traffic Service Team TEL. 032-741-2422~3
Outdoors Street Light Banners
  • Facility Description: Short-term lease for promotional banner facility for outdoor street lights at Incheon International Airport
  • Leasing Purpose: Banner installation for promoting international meetings and public events held in Republic of Korea
  • Location: Airport Expressway ~ Passenger Terminal vicinity
    • Outdoor street light banners will be approved after examining representativeness, publicity, and etc.
  • Installation Draft (Size & Design) for the use of outdoors street light banners needs to be submitted
    • Size: 600mm X 1,800mm
    • Self-made Built-in Banner Holder
  • Needs to be installed under the supervision of Woori Electric Power Repair Corporation
    • Power Repair Center TEL. 032-741-2821
  • After installation, maintenance is required through on-site inspection
    • Consider windy weather (it may fly or tear in strong winds)
  • Familiarize with User Precautions
Application Procedure
  • STEP1 Submit Events Facilities Application Form
    • Compose Reception Desk Terms of Use and Application Form Download
    • Compose Street Light Banners Terms of Use and Application Form Download
  • STEP2 After confirming whether it is possible to request a rental via phone, compose and submit an application form
    • TEL. 032-741-5850 (Events Facility Manager)
    • FAX. 032-741-2910 (After faxing, please call to confirm)
  • STEP3 After confirming the leasing purpose and desired date of leasing, will provide receipt and invoice
    • Cannot lease if leasing purpose does not match the criteria
    • Cannot be reserved and first-come-first-serve after receiving Application via Fax
  • STEP4 Notification of payment and payment within the due bate
    • Copy of Payment Receipt after the payment (FAX. 032-741-2370)
