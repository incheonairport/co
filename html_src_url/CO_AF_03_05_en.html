Incheon Airport does its best as a public facility by sharing with the client and lending a ear to the voice of our clients.

bbb campaign: Incheon Airport without a language barrier

bbb(before barbel brigade) is a pro bono interpretation service by volunteers who are fluent in a foreign language and the first of its kind in the world. As a global airport management enterprise, Incheon International Airport Corporation has worked with bbb Korea, Inc. since 2009 to build “Incheon Airport without a language barrier” through supporting bbb financially and promoting the bbb service including Incheon Airport bbb-day campaign during peak seasons. We also are working hard to lower the language barrier for both national and foreign clients by appointing about 300 outstanding interpreting volunteers to the Incheon Airport bbb special volunteer team.
