Stakeholder Participation

There are about 550 agencies and corporations within the Incheon International Airport, and the value of cooperation is the key to successful operation. Incheon International Airport Corporation separates the stakeholders into 5 categories: customer, business partner, local community, government, and employees. Each stakeholder category has a unique communication channel.

Through social responsibility management experience, Incheon International Airport Corporation seeks to listen to stakeholders and strive for a thriving and mutually beneficial relationship.
