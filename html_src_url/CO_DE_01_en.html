Academy Introduction

Overview

Incheon Airport Aviation Academy(IAAA) opened in 2008 to nurture the aviation professionals over the world. IAAA has a strong sense of belief that training must contribute to improving the trainees’ problems at works. To fulfill trainee’s authentic needs in training, IAAA courses are dynamic and practical consisted of best practice sessions, airport filed visits, case studies and workshops.

International Recognition

IAAA is one of the first training center designated as International Civil Aviation Organization(ICAO) Regional Training Center of Excellence. IAAA was certified as a TRAINAIR PLUS full member by ICAO in 2011. The certification acknowledges IAAA’s capabilities as worlds’ top level in developing and delivering competency-based training programs.

IAAA training programs are in conformity with the standards set by ICAO to guarantee the high quality of training, satisfying training needs.

Facilities

The 11-story building with the size of 73,000m² comprises various facilities for training, conference, accommodation and leisure. Aviation Training Center, located right beside the main building of IAAA, opened in 2013 for ATC training and security/safety training. Aviation Training Center provides aerodrome ATC training programs with cutting-edge facilities.

Classroom

Auditorium, 20 lecture rooms, computer laboratory, aviation security training room fully equipped with the most advanced audio-visual equipment.

Dormitories

102 furnished rooms with free internet access, TV, and self-service laundry available

Amenities

Indoor(fitness center, sauna, pool table, table tennis) and outdoor(football field, jogging track, basket ball court) facilities available
