Manifestation of a green Incheon Airport

In order to reach the vision of “2nd Flight as the Global Leader Airport”, Incheon International Airport Corporation has set a strategy of “Manifestation of a green airport” and is tirelessly working to set-up and maintain the Environment Management System to minimize the environmental effect of airport operation and construction. We propose the following policies for this purpose:

  • Abide by all environment-related laws, protocols, international treaties, and standards.
  • Reduce environment pollution from airport operation and construction and minimize environmental impact in the surrounding areas.
  • Adopt and enact environmental goals and improvement plans and operate a proactive and strategic Environment Management System through sustained outcome review.
  • Establish environment management transparency by providing related information to stakeholders.
  • Strengthen relationships with local community and stakeholders and practice environment-friendly airport operation and construction activities.

Policies outlined above applies to all departments, partners, auditors, and agencies within the Incheon International Airport Corporation, and all related persons must work to promote sustainable environment management model.
