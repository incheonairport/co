Cooperative program management

Incheon Airport Corporation is working to raise partner employee satisfaction and foster a positive cooperative relationship with our partner corporations through various mutually beneficial programs.

Assistance for partner employees family emergency
Raise sense of unity in times of family emergencies by providing basic needs items to Incheon Airport’s partner employees
Long-time employee domestic educational opportunities
Provide field trip and educational opportunities for Incheon Airport’s partner employees
Outstanding employee international opportunities
Provide overseas educational and cultural opportunities for Incheon Airport’s partner employees with outstanding track record
Rest area
Operate rest areas for partner employees to relieve stress and raise morale
Seasonal vaccination
Complementary flue vaccine for partner employees who come in contact with clients
Holiday gifts
Distribute holiday gifts (New Years, Thanksgiving) to encourage and thank partner employees
Support partner team-building events
Run athletic activities and other team-building events to build bonds between the within the partner organization
