CSV(Creating Shared Value)

To a promote sustainable business model, Incheon International Airport Corporation is practicing CSV(Creating Shared Value) through creating business value and social value in all aspects of management. IIAC incorporates the concept of CSV in strategy review and decision and practices CSR through linking CSV process to management strategy for CSV business discovery and promotion.

Key CSV businesses
Incheon Airport Culture-Port

365 days’. By providing a diverse cultural performance and experience programs, IIAC provide fun and emotional opportunity. We strive to develop the cultural business by providing space and opportunities to artists and promoting Korean culture both traditional and modern.

Industry cooperation field experience program

Incheon International Airport Corporation provides field experience programs during peak seasons and times to improve service efficiency. Students contribute to high quality service by helping customers in the terminals and assisting with smart services. The program provides a hands-on experience for students to consider a career in the airport sector, and it is an effort by Incheon Airport to provide the best service even in peak season and to contribute to society.

Export and technique sharing of environment-friendly aircraft with (AC-GPS)

Advisory Circular Global Positioning System (AC-GPS) is an operational machinery that converts voltage and wavelength to provide power supply to aircrafts in the berthage. IIAC has succeeded in working with middle-sized businesses to develop this technology and is at the center of expanding and improving the business. The technology cuts costs, and it also cuts down on the use of fuel and carbon dioxide emission, which creates social value. IIAC is working with smaller businesses to export AC-GPS products to overseas markets.

Job-creation through commercial facilities inside the airport

Through agreements with resident businesses, Incheon International Airport Corporation strives to create jobs in the airport. We strive to create senior positions and also to provide our customers with the service at a fair price in compliance with CSV.
