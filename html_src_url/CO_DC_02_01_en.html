Facility Introduction
  • Nest Hotel is a 1st class hotel located 15 minutes from Incheon International Airport, and it is a design hotel that was the first in Korea to be named a member of Design Hotels, a world renown hotel platform.
  • Nest Hotel has standard, deluxe, and suites for a total of 370 guest room, and all guest rooms are designed uniquely.
  • Nest Park, a beautiful harmony of sculpture and landscape along a wide field with the ocean as its backdrop, is spotlighted as a hidden gem of Nest Hotel, and you can leisurely enjoy a day on the beach, walk though the fields, or enjoy a perfect garden party.
