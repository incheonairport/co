Environment monitoring system

Incheon International Airport Corporation conducts 24-hour real time monitoring of the atmosphere, aircraft noise level, water quality, and interior air quality and monitors the output of wastewater facilities. Environment Monitoring Office , which is within the Aviation Industry Consultative Council (AICC), collects and analyzes real time data from each monitoring station to record and watch the environmental changes in Incheon Airport and nearby areas.

Environment monitoring system overview

Incheon International Airport has adopted the environment monitoring system in October, 2000 to continually monitor environment impact factors such as aircraft noise level and atmospheric quality, and we do our best to react to changes in the surrounding environmental through improvements and revising operations as needed.
