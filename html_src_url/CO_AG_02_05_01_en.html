Customer Charter

인천공항은 고객에게 더 나은 서비스를 제공하는 글로벌 리딩공항으로 도약하기 위해
아래와 같은 사항을 실천하겠습니다.

  • One. We will be a trusted airport that provides continual and flawless management to our customers.
  • One. We will create a convenience airport through timely procedures and pleasant atmosphere.
  • One. We will respond to customers complaint with kindness and timely response including revision and compensation.
  • One. We will actively listen to our customers’ comments as an open airport.

Incheon International Airport Corporation promises to diligently abide by the Service Practice Standards in order to implement this pledge.
