Change to highly-efficient LED lights

As a part of the energy conservation efforts, all of the lights in the airport are being changed to LED lights. The project began in 2009, and as of 2015, 65% of all lights in the airport have been changed to LED. There is continued investment to reach the goal of 100% LED light by 2020.

Climate change and energy conservation standard

Incheon International Airport is continually in the process of reducing energy-use. There are booklets for Incheon Airport workers (「Guidebook for Climate Change and Energy Conservation Efforts」 and 「Outstanding Cases of Incheon International Airport’s Energy Conservation Efforts」).
