Incheon International Airport Corporation received 3 international standard certification: ISO9001 quality management system and ISO14001 environmental management system in 2002 and ISO/IEC 20000 Certification in 2011.

World’s First Airport Service Category International Certification
ISO9001/140001 인증 획득

Incheon International Airport is the first airport management body in the world to receive ISO Certification with regards to airport service. By receiving ISO Certification in 9001(Quality) and 14001(Environment) Categories ISO Certification, Incheon International Airport has received an international endorsement for reaching outstanding standards in all areas of airport management in the short period of 2 years since the opening of the facilities. ISO9001 is a guideline about quality management system enhancement, and ISO14001 is a guideline about environmental management system. 

ISO : International Organization for Standardization

ISO9001/140001 인증취득 효과

Incheon International Airport’s management has been certified for following the international standard based on the 8 principles of quality management focused on customer satisfaction.  

Incheon International Airport Corporation is continually improving our work to follow the international standard in enhancing passenger comfort, and it means that our passengers can trust the service of the Corporation.

First Airport in Asia-Pacific to receive International IT Service Management System Certification

At the end of August 2011, Incheon International Airport Corporation became the first Asia-Pacific Airport to receive the ISO/IEC 20000 Certification. ISO/IEC 20000 is the global standard for IT service management system and operation, and it is given to the corporations that meet and hold the capacity of developed IT service management system and operation.

ISO/IEC 200000 인증 획득

ISO/IEC 20000은 IT서비스관리체계 및 운영에 관한 규격으로 글로벌 표준에 부합하는 선진 IT서비스관리체계 및 역량을 확보한 기업에게 수여됩니다.

ISO/IEC 20000 인증취득 효과

ISO/IEC 20000 Certification is effective IT service management that serves as the foothold of consistently high quality service system through earning international trust.
