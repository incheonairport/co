Airline Usage Contract

Contract for the Use of Incheon Airport

An airline wishing to launch a new service at Incheon Airport must enter into a contract for the use of Incheon Airport facilities prior to the commencement of service.

Procedure for Signing a Contract
  1. step 1

    Contract Signing Request
    (Official & Supporting Documents Submission)
    Airline → Airport Corporation

  2. step 2

    Send Security Deposit & Payment Request Form
    Airport corporation → Airline

  3. step 3

    Pay nSecurity Deposit
    Airline → Airport Corporation

  4. step 4

    Sign a Contract & Commence Service

Documents
  • Usage Agreement
  • Business Registration
  • Certified Copy of Corporation Registration
  • Certificate of Suitability of Airplane Registration and Noise Standard or Certificate of Airworthiness, etc. (MTOW Confirmation Information)
  • Flight Permit
  • User Flight Information Form Download
  • Customer Information Confirmation Download
  • Company (Airline) Introduction
  • Copy of Bank book (Only for passenger aircraft for payment of PSC collection fee)
  • Proof of Partnership between airline and contractor (GSA contract, etc.)
Security Deposit Payment Standard and Payment Method

The Security Deposit is calculated based on the three-month flight plan for landing fee, navigation facility use fee, airplane provision fee, public check-in counter fee, passenger airport fee, departure payment and international poverty eradication contribution. The Security Deposit must be paid by guaranteed insurance policy, bank guarantee, cash payment, etc. The warranty period will be extended, if the Security Deposit is not paid within two year from the start date of service.
