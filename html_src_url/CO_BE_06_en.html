Incheon Airport, Na-rea of National Happiness

『Incheon Airport, Wing of National Happiness』, published in celebration of ASQ achievement for 10 consecutive years, explains the secrets to exceptional customer service that made Incheon International Airport the 1st Airport in the world. There is specific analysis of the luxury customer service of Incheon International Airport, which is not just different but of exceptional quality. ‘Na-rea’ is a fictional character that walks the readers through the whole departure process at the airport, which is separated into sections of CURVESIDE, LANDSIDE, and AIRSIDE, and organizes all the luxury customer service that can be experienced at Incheon International Airport. The book is based on the ASQ research data, which specifies how Incheon Airport provides customer service and how it is different from other airports. The last chapter, NEXTSIDE, provides the future vision of Incheon International Airport, which has already obtained the world’s best title.

Distinguished Incheon Airport... What is Different?

This is one of the most beautiful success stories, which illustrates how International Airport has achieved and maintained its place at the top of the world only 10 years after its opening and being ranked at the top of World Airport Service Evaluation for 5 consecutive years.

The proceeds from book sales will be donated scholarship for talented students from the local community.
