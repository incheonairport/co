Issuance of Incheon Airport Protected Area Pass
  • A personnel or vehicle trying to access Incheon International Airport Protected Area or need to photoshoot, enroll in safety education and apply for the pass based on the procedure. Please consider the necessary time needed before following the instruction.
  • Please refer to the pass application system and leaflet for more details from pass application to Aviation Security Compliance.
