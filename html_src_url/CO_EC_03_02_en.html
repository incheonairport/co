Cases Not Applicable for On-site Sales Approval Application Procedure

In the following cases, you can conduct business without going through the On-site Approval Application Procedure.

If it is unclear whether or not it is applicable, please confirm with the Corporation.

Approval Inclusion, Approval Exclusion Classification Criteria

구내 영업승인 승인간주에 대한 표입니다.
On-site Sales Approval Include
  • A person who has entered into a contract or is scheduled to enter into a contract to provide goods and services to the Corporations.
  • A person who has concluded or is to conclude a contract, which includes the consideration for leasing a commercial facility, etc.
  • A person who performs business by acquiring or completing licenses, declarations, etc. in accordance with laws and regulations such as the aviation law, and who pays cost equivalent to on-site sales operation fee to the Corporation
On-site Sales Approval Exclude

A person who falls under any of the following subcategories uses an airport facility managed and operated by the Corporation without care

  • A person who obtains permission to move into a Free Trade Zone pursuant to the Act on the Designation and Operation of Free Trade Zones and operates mainly outside the airport facilities managed and operated by the Corporation.
  • A person who provides goods or services related to the convenience and welfare of regular employees
  • A person who installs, maintains, repairs facilities owned or used by a Resident Employee (institution)
  • A person who supplies goods necessary for the operation of a commercial facility to a person who has entered into a leasing contract with the Corporation
  • A person who has other reasons similar to the above
