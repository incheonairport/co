Incheon Airport Scholarship

Incheon International Airport Corporation provides Incheon Airport Scholarship to outstanding high school students and professionals in the field of aviation to foster future talents.

Incheon Airport Scholarship Type

  • Learning Scholarship: outstanding high school students from local community
  • Future Scholarship: academically outstanding high school students from local community
  • Global Leader Scholarship: outstanding undergraduate or graduate students studying in the field related to aviation and distribution

Support the construction and operation of Incheon Sky High School

In order to foster a residential environment around Incheon Airport and to enhance educational environment in the community, Incheon International Airport Corporation established an independent private school, Incheon Sky High School, which opened in March 2011, and continues to sponsor the school.

Incheon Sky High School support

  • Construction cost: 48.9 billion KRW
  • Operations cost: 1.5 – 2.5 billion KRW annually

World Peace Forest

IIAC has partnered with the Forest for Life, a nonprofit, to create the “World Peace Forest” (surface areas: 472,910㎡) and has supported the community members to maintain the forest in an effort to create an urban environmental where human and nature coexist and community members can gather.

Support for the construction and operation of Sky Cultural Center

IIAC built the “Sky Cultural Center”, which opened in March 2012, for community members in Yeongjong and Yuyong area who cannot easily access the cultural benefits of a big city. The Sky Cultural Center has become a place where people around the airport can enjoy a variety of cultural and athletic activities and can spend time together.
