Monitoring System

We operate a monitoring system to prevent any violation of fair-trade laws.

Monitoring Process
Preliminary Monitoring
    • Business Department’s Request for Review

      Documents for review(contract, scope of work, proposal request, sales form)

    • Business Department creates checklist and attachment

    Send cooperation request
    • Legal Team Review

      Reply with result of the review

    • Legal Team collects documents on the checklist

    • When revision is not necessary When revision is necessary
    • Business Department’s Request for Review
  4. Legal Team Review
  5. Inspect implementation of revision
Follow-up Monitoring
  1. Business Department

    Create and send checklist of contract’s implementation steps (twice a year)

  2. Legal Team

    Collect and review checklist of implementation steps

  3. Inspect implementation of revision
Preliminary Monitoring

Through a self-check list and internal legal review in the contract-formation stage, reduce possibility of violating the law with regards to fair-trade.

Follow-up Monitoring

Inspect compliance of fair-trade regulation in the contract implementation process, and quickly revise in the case of any violation.

Regular Monitoring

Twice a year, collect and analyze preliminary and follow-up monitoring results to evaluate CP management outcome and suggest any improvements.
