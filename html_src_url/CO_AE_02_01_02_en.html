Ethical Management Practices

At Incheon International Airport Corporation, Social Responsibility (SR) Committee formed around the CEO makes key decisions regarding ethics and social responsibility management, and each department selects an ethic officer to enhance ethic culture. In order to strengthen the implementation of ethical management, there is an integrity team, formally called the Anti-Corruption Promotion Team.

Director
  • CEO
  • Standing Auditor
Deliberating body
  • SR Committee
    • Head: CEO
    • Members: Management Team
    • Adviser : Mutual Growth Director
  • Anti-Corruption Promotion Team
    • Head: Standing Auditor
    • Members: Planning Director, Business Innovation Director, Mutual Growth Director, Business Support Director, Business Facility Director, Passenger Service Director, Construction Management Director, Terminal Facility Director, Flight Support Director
    • Adviser: Audit Assistant Manager
