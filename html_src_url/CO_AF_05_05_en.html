Incheon Airport Atmospheric Environment Management

We operate a comprehensive atmospheric quality management system, which considers atmospheric waste production, vehicles, and aircrafts.

Mobile pollution source

In order to minimize atmosphere pollution, we conduct a regular inspection of mobile pollution sources such as aircraft and ground equipment used in the airport facilities.

Point pollution source 
  • In the case of incineration facilities that produce large quantities of atmosphere pollutants, there is a exhaust fume management system to contain waste gases and operate a Clean-Plant. 
  • In the case of the gas-fired combine cycle combustion turbine plant, we use LNG, a clean fuel with almost no atmosphere pollution, and even in the case of emergency, we use low-sulfur diesel to minimize the emission of atmosphere pollutants.
  • We built the incineration facilities and the gas-fired combine cycle combustion turbine plant in the same area, so that the waste heat from the incinerators can be reused in the plant.
