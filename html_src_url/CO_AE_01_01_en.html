Sustainability

Incheon International Airport Corporation (IIAC) was established to contribute to the national economic development and improvement of air transit through effective construction, maintenance, and management of the Incheon International Airport. IIAC successfully opened the Incheon Airport in March, 2001, and through continuous innovation, it has made the Incheon Airport the world-renown top-tier hub airport of East Asia. Incheon Airport went from serving 14 million people in the first year, 2001, to 58 million passengers in 2016 (7th in the world) and from processing 1.2 million tons of cargo in 2001 to 2.7 million tons in 2016 (2nd in the world). Through a distinguished passenger service and customer-centered business model, Incheon Airport has ranked number 1 for 12 consecutive years in World Airport Service Evaluation, a record for the history of airport sector.

Based on these achievements, Incheon International Airport Corporation will manifest the dream of a sustainable leading business that represents Korea through innovative management, trust among stakeholders, passion, and capacity.
