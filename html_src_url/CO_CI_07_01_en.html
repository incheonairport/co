Real-name Business System

Real-name Business System is a way to make public to citizens the real name of the head of each public agency, its key business and direction forward, and Incheon International Airport Corporation has implemented the Real-name Business System since 2015 to strengthen business integrity and transparency.

Real-name Business System Eligible Business
  1. A. Service businesses that are deemed to be closely related to transportation of passenger and cargo
  2. B. Large businesses with huge financial impact(with over 10 billion)
  3. C. Businesses related to key government issues
  4. D. And other key businesses that require central attention(normalization of public agency, criticisms from exterior agencies, attention from media or public, etc.)
