TIP Summary

TIP(Tailored Incentive Program)

Airline oversea branches can apply the customized incentive scheme as means of business, which provides benefits according to the customer’s needs. In order to attract ‘transit passengers’ via Incheon, additional benefits such as customized transit tours, duty-free shops, discount coupons for food, beverage, and lounge can be provided.

Application and Procedure are the following:

  • 1.Airline Application

    TIP Application from the airport’s homepage, at a local travel agency
    (buyer), or at the local branch of airline’s departure point

  • 2.Airport Corporation Acceptance & Acceptance

    Determine the propriety and strategy to attract and support based on the contents of Application Form

  • 3.Detailed Consultation

    When selecting a finalist, the airline or a travel agency should be consulted to compose details of a support plan

  • 4.Provide Incentives

    Provide and implement incentives

  • 5.Calculations & Report Results

    Calculations of incurred expenses and Report the Evaluation(Airline/Travel Agency -> Airport Corporation)
