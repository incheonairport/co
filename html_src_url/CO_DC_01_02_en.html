Incheon International Airport unused land investment conditions
  • Incheon International Airport development method: land is the property of the corporation, and the facility is developed and managed by a private entity
  • Land-use fee: 5/100 of appraised land value(separately negotiate additional conditions or reductions)
  • Land-use period: within 50 years from beginning of operation(separately negotiate extension)
  • Related laws
    • Metropolitan Area New Airport Construction Promotion Act
    • Free Economic Zone Designation and Management Act
  • Proposed facility
    • Prosed as airport support facility as in Metropolitan Area New Airport Construction Promotion Act  (Restriction: height limit EL 52m)
  • Business implementer selection process(negotiate details with Corporation)
    • Business implementer recruitment announcement > accept proposals > evaluation and priority negotiation subject designation > sign enforcement agreement > business implementer designation > drafting, licensing, and construction
