Leasing a Commercial Facility

Commercial Facilities Lease
  • Rental space used for sales activities such as duty-free shops, food and beverage stores, specialty stores, etc. located at Incheon Airport
  • In accordance with the National Contract Law, the operator is selected through open bidding.
  • Bid Announcement
  • Business Briefing
  • Bid Participation
  • Winner Selection
  • Successful Bidder Notification
  • Payment of Rental Deposit
  • Sign a Contract
Procedure for Contract Conclusion

The contract is concluded via electronic contract, and it is necessary to join the electronic bidding system (If a member of a local market, no need to sign up separately)

Commercial Facility Rent Calculation Method

Comparing collection, operating rate, rental rate, fixed rent, calculation method according to passenger increase rate

Commercial Facility Lease Payment Method
  • Payment is made on the 7th day of each month, must be paid by the 20th
  • When paid late, 16% annual payment increase from the payment period to the expiration date
  • Electronic payment available
